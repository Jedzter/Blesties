Demon Slayer: 10 Billion Yen in Box Office

Within the first ten days of release, Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba THE MOVIE Mugen Train has grossed over 10 billion yen ($95 million), making it the fastest film in history to do so. This film now holds the records for the best weekend, the second-best weekend, and the best second weekend, surpassing the previous title holder, Spirited Away.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba THE MOVIE Mugen Train was able to beat Spirited Away's 19 year reign in less than half the time it took Spirited Away (25 days) to gross 10 million yen. Other great Japanese films such as Your Name and Weathering With You grossed 10 million yen in 28 days and 34 days respectively.

My Hero Academia: Ultra Impact Mobile Game Beta Testing

My Hero Academia: Ultra Impact is a mobile game based on Kohei Horikoshi's popular series, My Hero Academia! The game is now ready to face beta testing where Android users are able to sign up on their site to participate in a closed beta. This will be held between November 6 and 9, where about 7,000 users will be able to experience the game first hand.

These are a few screenshots of the game. Players can expect to see base customization, battles, and the ability to roll for characters using the game's gacha system. Eventually, there will also be a full story mode to the game. My Hero Academia: Ultra Impact can be expected to launch sometime next year on IOS and Android.
